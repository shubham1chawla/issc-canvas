Week 2

In this module, international faculty and senior students will share their experiences and insights on meeting academic expectations and navigating daily life in the U.S. Students will also gain practical knowledge about essential financial matters, including opening a bank account, applying for a credit card, managing personal finances, exploring investment options, and learning how to recognize and avoid scams. Together, these sessions are designed to help students transition more confidently into both academic and everyday life at ASU.

Assignment
Classroom Behavior

Classroom behavior, expectations, norms, and guidelines for student success

Terminologies

Understand the commonly used terms around ASU campuses by international students

Banking

Learn important information regarding banking in the U.S.

Scams

Help protect yourself from scams that target international students.
