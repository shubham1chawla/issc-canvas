Unit 2

This unit is designed to support your transition into the U.S. academic system and set you up for success at ASU. You’ll learn how to access academic advising, explore essential tools like MyASU and iPos, Canvas, and discover key academic and research resources including programs like MORE and TA/ RA/ GSA roles. We’ll also cover the concept of academic integrity, including what it means in the U.S. academic context and the consequences of violating it. The aim of this module is to help you make the most of your academic experience at ASU.
