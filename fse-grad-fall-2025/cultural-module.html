Cultural Adjustment

Adjusting to a new culture can be both exciting and challenging. No matter how much preparation you've done, there will likely be moments that surprise you. To help ease your transition, we've compiled this module, which we hope will make your experience smoother and more enjoyable.

Please keep in mind that all advice should be considered as general guidance. The United States is a vast and diverse country, and cultural norms can vary significantly depending on the state, region, or individual family background. While this guide highlights common aspects of American culture, it's important to remember that not everyone will fit neatly into these generalizations. Instead, think of this as a starting point to help you navigate and adapt to life as a graduate student in the U.S.
