Week 1

This module will help students build a strong foundation for their journey at ASU by introducing them to important immigration regulations and policies they need to be aware of. Students will also have the opportunity to meet Global Peer Mentors (GPMs) and representatives from various clubs and organizations, gaining valuable insights on how to connect with the campus community, get involved in activities, and make the most of their time at ASU. In addition, the module highlights key community and social resources that can support students as they adjust to life in the U.S.
