Week 3

In Week 3, students will learn about academic advising at ASU and how Academic Advisors play a vital role in supporting graduate success. This session will provide guidance on planning coursework, using tools like iPOS to track degree progress, and maintaining good academic standing. Students will also hear directly from an Academic Advisor about the advising process and the many ways they can assist throughout the graduate journey.
