Week 5

This session focuses on academic integrity and the responsible use of AI in graduate studies. Faculty will provide guidance on how to use AI tools ethically and effectively while upholding academic standards. In addition, the Academic Integrity Officer will share real examples of violations, explain the investigation process, and discuss the potential impact such cases can have on academic records. Together, these insights will help students understand expectations and make informed, responsible choices in their academic work.
