Career Advancement

This unit is designed to help you explore career pathways, understand job search strategies, and learn how to make the most of ASU's career development resources. You'll connect with international alumni who have successfully navigated their career journeys and gain insights into internships, full-time opportunities, and career services. You'll also learn the key details about CPT and OPT, including eligibility, timelines, and best practices for planning your career as an international student.
