Welcome to Week 2!

This module will introduce students to the essential elements of the U.S. education system, ensuring a smooth transition into academic life at ASU. Students will gain a clear understanding of classroom behavior expectations, communication norms with instructors, and the broader educational structure that shapes their learning environment. Through engaging resources and discussions, participants will develop skills to confidently navigate their courses and succeed in their academic journey.

To learn more about finances, scholarship opportunities, and tuition fee payments, check out the Finances module (also available under Supplementary Modules, which offer additional but useful insights).
