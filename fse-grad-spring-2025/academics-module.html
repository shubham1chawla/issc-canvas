Welcome to Week 4!

This module provides a comprehensive overview of key resources and essential information for international graduate students at ASU. Designed to ensure a smooth transition and successful academic journey, the module covers critical aspects such as maintaining academic integrity, managing tuition payments, exploring scholarship opportunities, navigating the MyASU portal, and utilizing the Canvas. Each course is tailored to address the unique needs of international students, helping them integrate seamlessly into the ASU community and make the most of their educational experience.
