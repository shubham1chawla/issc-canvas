Learning about the Grand Canyon State

This module offers a unique and immersive opportunity for international students to explore and engage with the vibrant culture, history, and natural beauty of the Grand Canyon State. Whether you're a new arrival or have been here for some time, this module will provide valuable insights into the diverse communities, landscapes, and traditions that make Arizona a truly special place.

Through a blend of interactive lectures, field trips, and discussions, you will gain a deeper understanding of Arizona's rich heritage and contemporary life, fostering a sense of belonging and connection. Join us as we embark on a journey to uncover the essence of Arizona through the eyes of international students, fostering cultural exchange, and enriching your academic experience.
