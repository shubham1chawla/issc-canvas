International Arrival

International Welcome will be held on Monday, August 19, 2024 for graduate students. All new graduate and transfer international students are required to attend International Welcome. This in-person event offers informative and interactive programs, and an overview of a wide variety of topics to help you learn more and prepare for life at ASU.

Registered students and families will receive additional details and the full event schedule through email. Students who register for International Welcome will also receive communication about free airport transportation provided by Uber.
