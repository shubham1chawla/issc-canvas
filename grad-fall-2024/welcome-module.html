International Arrival

International Welcome will be held at your home campus near the start of the semester. All graduate students are expected to attend the program. Check your email for the registration link to the event on your campus. This in-person event offers informative and interactive programs, and an overview of a wide variety of topics to help you learn more and prepare for life at ASU.

Register for the International Welcome on your campus

  • Aug. 11 — A Pre-Welcome for ALL international students at the Tempe campus.
  • Aug. 16 — Student arrival in the U.S., recommended for Polytechnic students.
  • Aug. 17. — International Welcome at the Polytechnic campus
  • Aug. 18 — Student arrival in the U.S., recommended for Tempe graduate, West Valley and Downtown Phoenix students.
  • Aug. 20 — International Welcome at the Tempe campus
  • Aug. 20 — International Welcome at the Downtown Phoenix campus
  • Aug. 20 — International Welcome at the West Valley campus
  • Aug. 21 — First day of the fall semester
