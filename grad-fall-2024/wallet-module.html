Welcome Wallet

Welcome to your ASU International Student Welcome Wallet! This collection of resources is designed to help you prepare for your arrival and transition to life at Arizona State University. Here you'll find important presentations and information on topics like orientation, health services, counseling, and more. Be sure to review these materials as they contain crucial details for your successful start at ASU. For additional pre-arrival information, including webinars, checklists, and key dates, please refer to the Welcome and Pre-Arrival module.
