Welcome graduate students!

Welcome to ASU!

We here at the International Students and Scholar Center (also known as the ISSC) want to make your first semester a little easier to navigate. Therefore, we have set up this Canvas resource hub filled with resources for you to discover. We will release modules with new information on an ongoing basis, so watch your email for Canvas updates from us.

Please use the discussion board to talk with each other, ask questions and communicate with us. We are here to answer questions, get to know you and here to help you make ASU your new home.

Please use this calendar to find ISSC events and add them to your own calendar. Event registration links can be found in the descriptions.
