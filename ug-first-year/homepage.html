Welcome to ASU!

We here at the International Students and Scholar Center (also known as the ISSC) want to make your first semester a little easier to navigate. Therefore, we have set up this Canvas course filled with resources for you to discover. We will release modules with new information on an ongoing basis, so watch your email for Canvas updates from us.

Please use the discussion board to talk with each other, ask questions and communicate with your Global Peer Mentors, who are where to answer questions, get to know you and here to help you make ASU your new home.
