Life at Campus

ASU is a large school with many different campuses, but just because it is so large doesn't mean you need to feel lost. In fact, we hope all the options and resources here at ASU provide you with the community, help and support you need to not only excel at school, but to find those friends that make ASU feel like home.

This module is here to help you find events, student groups/orgs to make yourself a loving, supporting community, PLUS all those ASU departments that can help and support you.

Always use our discussion board to ask questions about things you can find here but wish to find more about.
