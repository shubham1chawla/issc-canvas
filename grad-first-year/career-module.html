Global Professional Preparation Academy (GPPA)

We know many of you are already interested in getting a job on campus, therefore the ISSC has developed the Global Professional Preparation Academy, better known as the GPPA.

The GPPA is a semester-long series for first-year international students to build their professional portfolios through workshops hosted by the ISSC, Career and Professional Development Services, and the W.P. Carey School of Business.

The GPPA is designed and structured to aid students in achieving both academic and career success through trainings that will give you the A-Z on how to create your professional profile. The GPPA will have a resume, cover letter, interview, and networking workshops, plus much more.

The focus of this program will be to help students gain the skills needed for successfully applying to on-campus jobs, with additional support on building their long-term professional development.

This program is designed to help our first-year international students, therefore extensive event information will be launched on the canvas resource hub starting August 16th. So please settle in and look forward to this module and all the content we will have to offer - not to mention the workshops and events!

ISSC Volunteering

We're looking for globally minded, service-oriented students who would like to volunteer at events and in our offices. The event volunteer assistant is expected to offer excellent customer service in person, set up and run events, assist with check-in, and more. If you have attention to detail, are flexible, looking to build your resume and have multitasking skills, this is the position for you.

Some volunteer experience might be you just attending the event and being engaged with the other students attending by making sure they feel welcome to being on an event committee to help us plan some of our larger events!
